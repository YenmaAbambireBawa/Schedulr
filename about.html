About Schedulr

Transforming course registration for Ashesi University students through intelligent automation and innovative technology solutions.

Built by Students, For Students

Schedulr was born from the frustration of manual course registration processes. As Ashesi students ourselves, we experienced firsthand the stress of competing for limited seats, managing schedule conflicts, and navigating complex registration procedures.

We realized that technology could eliminate these pain points. What started as a simple automation script evolved into a comprehensive platform that now serves the entire Ashesi community.

Today, Schedulr processes thousands of course registrations each semester, saving students countless hours and reducing registration-related stress to near zero. Our mission is to ensure every Ashesi student can focus on learning, not on administrative logistics.
